Who We Are

Over 40 years of hands-on experience in the equestrian industry

Ox Gear isn’t just a brand — it’s a legacy built on the racetrack. What began in the early 1980s with hands-on work on the backside has grown into a trusted name in equestrian gear. Founded by Rick Alexander, a lifelong horseman, Ox Gear brings together decades of experience and a deep understanding of what riders truly need.

We specialize in custom, high-performance tack made from modern materials like Beta — stronger, cleaner, and longer-lasting than leather. Whether it's halters, bridles, or saddle pads, everything we create is designed with purpose, made to perform, and built to last.
